This discovery phase helps ensure future initiatives address the areas where change will create the greatest impact. ![Image 10: Close](blob:http://localhost/56201ade8e58dba5cfc6098c1ff6d56a)

By focusing on modular, reusable structures, each initiative becomes a building block that supports future innovation and long-term scalability. ![Image 13: Close](blob:http://localhost/56201ade8e58dba5cfc6098c1ff6d56a)
